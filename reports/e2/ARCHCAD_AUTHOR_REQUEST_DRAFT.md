## 본문 (영문 — 학계 관행)

Dear Dr. Zhang and Dr. Zhao,

I am writing regarding your NeurIPS 2025 dataset paper, "ArchCAD-400K: An Open Large-Scale
Architectural CAD Dataset and New Baseline for Panoptic Symbol Spotting" (arXiv:2503.22346).
Congratulations on the work — the primitive-level, five-modality alignment is exactly the kind
of resource our research needs.

We are conducting non-commercial academic research on semantic detection of walls and related
primitives in architectural CAD drawings. We have already downloaded and are using the public
40K subset released on Hugging Face (jackluoluo/ArchCAD), and we fully respect its CC BY-NC 4.0
terms.

Your paper describes the full corpus as 413,062 chunks from 5,538 standardized drawings with an
official drawing-isolated 7:1:2 split. The public README indicates the 40K release is a first
batch, with subsequent releases planned. Could you let us know:

1. Whether the full ArchCAD-400K corpus (or a larger portion than the 40K subset) can be made
   available to us for non-commercial academic research, and under what terms;
2. If so, the preferred access method (download link, gated form, or direct transfer); and
3. Whether the official drawing-isolated split manifest and the per-chunk physical scale
   (14 m x 14 m) metadata are included, so that we can preserve project-level isolation and
   metric coordinates.

We are happy to complete any access form, sign a non-commercial use agreement, or provide
institutional details as needed.

Thank you very much for releasing this dataset and for considering our request.

Best regards,
