## Correction recorded (no-fake honesty)

`research/native_arx/acdb-core.md` cites `AcDbDatabase::getSysVar(...)` / `getLUNITS` for
`inspect.database.sysvar` and marks the signature **unverified**. ObjectARX 2027 `dbmain.h`
has **no `getSysVar` and no `getLUNITS`** (header scan: 0 occurrences). `inspect.database.sysvar`
is implemented as a curated **read** over the real typed accessors
(`lunits`/`insunits`/`measurement`/`getFingerprintGuid`) plus the global `acedGetVar` for an
arbitrary named var; the catalog's `write_copy` (`setSysVar`) path is intentionally not built.
